Our Story

A Legacy of Welcome

The White Rhino Hotel was established in 1910 by Berkely Cole, Lord Cranworth and Sandy Herd. Through decades of change, the hotel evolved from a colonial outpost into a symbol of unity and welcome for all. In 2001 it was declared a National Heritage Site, honouring its enduring place in Kenya's story.

Our Journey Through Time
