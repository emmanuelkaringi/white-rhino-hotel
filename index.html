Our Heritage

A Story Over a Century in the Making

Founded in 1910, The White Rhino Hotel has stood as a landmark of hospitality in Nyeri for over a hundred years. From colonial origins to a proudly recognized National Heritage Site, our doors have welcomed generations of travellers seeking warmth, comfort and timeless character.
