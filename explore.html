Explore Nyeri

Discover Nyeri's Finest

History, nature and adventure await just beyond our doors in the heart of the highlands.

Aberdare National Park

Misty forests, waterfalls and abundant wildlife across the dramatic Aberdare ranges.

Baden-Powell Museum

The final resting place and legacy of the founder of the Scout Movement.

Italian War Memorial Church

A poignant stone memorial honouring Italian prisoners of war.

Mau Mau Caves

Historic forest hideouts central to Kenya's struggle for independence.

Nyeri Museum

Regional cultural exhibits telling the story of Nyeri and its people.

The White Rhino Hotel

A historic 4-star heritage hotel in Nyeri, welcoming guests with warmth and character since 1910.
